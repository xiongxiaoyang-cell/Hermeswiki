# Australia (AUS) — Labour Policy

## Minimum Wage
- National MW: AUD 24.73/hour (since July 2025)
- Annual review by Fair Work Commission
- Next review: July 2026, expected +3-4%
- Award rates may be higher than national MW

## Key Institutions
- Fair Work Commission (FWC)
- Fair Work Ombudsman
