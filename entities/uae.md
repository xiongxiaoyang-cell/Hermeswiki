# United Arab Emirates (ARE) — Labour Policy

## Labour Law
- Federal Decree-Law No. 33/2021
- No statutory national minimum wage
- MOHRE requires employers to provide "reasonable compensation"

## Workforce
- Expat workers: 90%+ of workforce
- Anti-discrimination provisions strengthened
- Flexible work arrangements introduced
